OwnTracks apps

The OwnTracks apps operate in one of three modes (discussed in scenarios], and you tell the app in which mode you want to use it.

Public mode is the easiest: you don't have to configure anything. Next up is Hosted mode, in which you configure your username, your device name, and the access token you got when signing up to Hosted OwnTracks.

In Private mode, you configure our iOS and Android app to access your broker.
